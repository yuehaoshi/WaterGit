# WaterGit
 WaterGit, a community for water engineers and water-lovers to share their water experience!
 
<img width="969" alt="Screenshot 2023-01-11 at 19 20 34" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70357591/211968120-71132461-99f3-4e08-b66f-b55c28fa43ef.png">

Here, you can post your experience about one city, or comment in one existing city water review. There is also a cluster map showing on the top of the post page where you can select your city of interest easily.  

<img width="1424" alt="Screenshot 2023-01-11 at 19 29 41" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70357591/211969182-39826859-5aff-4984-bcca-0aa3aff463c6.png">

To post or comment, you must log-in or register as a new user:

<img width="1415" alt="Screenshot 2023-01-11 at 19 31 46" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/70357591/211969409-2356f333-c5e0-4e91-b7fe-6ff3dc9879a7.png">


How to use:
- Clone this repository into your local space
- Create a new `.env` file containing your mapBox, Cloudynary and MongoDB token
- run `npm start`, and enjoy!

Welcome to contact me(yuehaoshi@outlook.com) if you have any questions or ideas!
